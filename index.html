About

ELfreediving (엘프리다이빙) is a freediving organization based in Incheon, South Korea. We build software and provide services for the freediving community — most notably Apnea Comp, an all-in-one platform for organizers, judges, and staff to run AIDA-sanctioned freediving competitions.

Apnea Comp

Run freediving competitions end to end:

  • Start lists & live countdown
  • Judging, penalties & results (AIDA Rulebook)
  • Records (WR/CR/NR) tracking
  • Real-time sync across the whole team
  • Apple Watch safety companion
